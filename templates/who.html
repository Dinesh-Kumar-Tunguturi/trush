Who ApplyWizz is helping:

At ApplyWizz, our Match Score is designed for ambitious abroad job seekers ready to secure their next opportunity. We work with:

  • Straight out of college students
  • Career switchers
  • Recent graduates
  • Professionals with career gaps
We analyze your complete profile — resume, LinkedIn, LeetCode, GitHub, portfolio, and other professional links — to see how well you align with your target role. Our insights pinpoint exactly what’s strong, what needs improvement, and how to transform missed chances into interview calls.
