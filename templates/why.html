Why ApplyWizz

ApplyWizz’s Match Score platform gives job seekers a clear, data-backed view of their complete professional profile. By analyzing details from your LinkedIn, LeetCode, GitHub, portfolio, and more, it measures how closely you match your chosen domain and role in abroad. In seconds, you get a precision score plus actionable suggestions to improve visibility and relevance. Unlike generic tools, ApplyWizz delivers insights aligned with real recruiter expectations, so you know exactly where you stand and how to stand out. It’s your fastest path to turning an online profile into interview opportunities.
